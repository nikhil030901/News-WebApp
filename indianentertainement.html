    • About our website
    • We discovered the problem that our peer group was not in the habit of news reading or following up with the latest events and they found the reason was simply that the lengthy news articles were not engaging enough. So we found Newsconnect to provide relevant news articles for users to quickly consume the most relevant content of the day and stay up to date with the relevant news. We wanted to provide a website to our users where they can read all the news directly without subscribing and get all the information from different websites. We are also not asking them to sign up on our website because we want our website to be users friendly.
